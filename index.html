About Troop 111

Welcome to the website of Troop 111! We are a Boy Scouts of America troop in Fremont, CA. We are part of the Mission Peak district, and in the San Francisco Bay Area Council. The troop meets every Monday in John Gomes Elementary School, when the Mission schools are in session. Troop 111 has been serving the community for over 50 years.

Interested in becoming a Troop 111 Scout? Boys between the ages of 11 and 18 are welcome to join us.
